Herr B loses against Herr M (H). In all encounters between those two, Herr M emerges victorious. Herr S (H) is a more challenging opponent for Herr M (V). Here, Herr S is defeated in a close game.
Herr S (H) is the stronger player in the one-on-one matchup against Herr B (V). As is the case for all games where Herr B starts, Herr S wins this game. In the case that Herr S (V) begins, the matchup is fairly even. The above game is won by Herr B (H).
