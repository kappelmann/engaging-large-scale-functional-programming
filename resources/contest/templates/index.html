Welcome to the FPV Programming Contest!

The FPV Programming contest is hosted by your tutors!



Guidelines:

You will compete in teams of two to three students.

Do not share your results with other competitors - this will disqualify you.



Scoring:

The contest consists of a total of 7 problem statements.

Each problem is graded as follows:

Fail if the submitted solution does not satisfy the desired functionality.

Correct if the submitted solution satisfies the desired functionality but has bad runtime.

Pass if the solution meets our runtime requirements and satisfies the desired functionality.

The Team with the most passes wins.

Your job is to pass as many problems as quickly as you can!

If there is a tie, we consider correct but inefficient solutions next.

If there still is a tie, we consider the team with lower latest timestamp.

If we then still have a tie, we call it a tie! :)



Grade bonus:

If you get passes in a minimum of 3 out of the 7 problems, you will earn a coin for this week.



Let's start the Contest!
